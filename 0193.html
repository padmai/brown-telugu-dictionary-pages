ఏ  The pronoun or sign of question; interrogative adjective. Which, what. Thus ఏ చెట్టు which tree? ఏ పిల్లలు what children? or, if అయినా is affixed to the word with which it is used, it is translated any: as ఏ చెట్టయినా, ఏ పిల్లలయినా any tree, any children.
   ఏ  ē. The long vowel E. as a in fare.

   ఏ  ē. An affix having a demonstrative force. అతడే నాయన్న, there! that man is my brother.
   ఏ  ē. An affix denoting that a female is addressed ఏమే. O woman!
   ఏ  ē. An affix denoting denial or negation. అది నీకు తగునే does it befit you? no.
   ఏ  ē. An affix denoting 'only'; by oneself, without others' help. నేనే పోతిని I alone went. వాని యింటికి మా యింటికి ఒక గోడే అడ్డము there is only a wall between his house and mine. ఆ లెక్కను నేనే చేసితిని I did that sum myself.
   ఏ  ē. An affix denoting emphasis: thus, నేనే I myself. వాడే that very man. మేమే we ourselves. This is sometimes doubled. Thus అతడేనే he himself.
