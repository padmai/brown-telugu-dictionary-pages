ద్వైతము  dvaitamu. [Skt.] n. Dualism; the theory that there are two eternal and self-existing substances or principles. Opposed to అద్వైతము which teaches that there is but one substance of which all things are manifestations. Also the name of a forest, ఒకానొకవనము.
